About Us





Welcome!

Whether you're in the mood for something sweet or salty, we've got something for you at our locally owned and operated cafe and artisan bakery. To us, it's all about creating memories in an inviting, cosy, and chic environment for foodies to share their passions.

We put our heart and soul into everything we serve.Whether it's the wholesome artisanal breads, pastries, and cakes, or the freshly brewed coffee, we've put everything we've got into making something special for everyone. There is no greater reward for us than witnessing the delight on our guests' faces when they take their first bite of something from our thoughtfully crafted menu.

Coffee Making
Our brewers and pourers or more known as barista, are more than just craftsmen they are artists whose creations power our customers daily lives. Not only does our baristas make the best cappucinos and most refreshing iced coffees customers ever tasted, but they know everything there is to know about the machines and brewing methods that go into every single cup. Drinks are made, recipes are devised and here at Brew'Tiful Cakery, our baristas can make coffee exactly to your specifications!
